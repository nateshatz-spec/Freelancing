# Day 2: High-Intent Outreach Scripts

Use these templates to reach out to potential clients. Remember to **personalize** the bracketed sections.

## 1. The "Cinematic MVP" Pitch (Twitter/X or LinkedIn DM)
**Target:** Seed-stage founders or builders in public.

> "Hey [Name], just saw your latest update on [Project Name]. The concept is incredible, but I noticed the current UI might not be doing the vision justice yet. 
> 
> I specialize in building 'Cinematic MVPs'—high-fidelity, premium digital experiences for wellness brands. Just finished a spec demo of a wellness dashboard that might fit your vibe: [Link to your showcase]
> 
> Any interest in a quick 10-min chat about elevating your app's visual identity to match the quality of your tech? No pressure, just thought I'd reach out."

---

## 2. The "UI/UX Rescue" Offer (Email or LinkedIn)
**Target:** Existing apps that look a bit dated or cluttered.

> "Hi [Name],
> 
> I’ve been following [Company Name] for a bit and love what you're doing for [Problem they solve]. 
> 
> I’m a high-fidelity developer currently looking for my next boutique project. I noticed a few areas in the [App Name] onboarding flow where some cinematic polish could really drive up user retention.
> 
> I'd love to send over a free 3-slide 'UI Rescue' mockup for your main dashboard. If you like the direction, we can talk about a full sprint. If not, the mockups are yours to keep.
> 
> Cheers,
> [Your Name]"

---

## 3. The "Founders in Public" Reply
**Target:** Replying to tweets/posts where founders complain about dev speed or design.

> "I feel this. Most MVPs look like templates because they are. If you're looking to move away from the 'standard' look and want something cinematic that actually converts, I've got a slot opening up next week. Here's what I'm building right now: [Link to your showcase]"

---

## Best Practices for Day 2:
1.  **Quantity + Quality:** Aim for 20-30 messages. Each one should take 2-3 minutes to personalize.
2.  **Focus on the Vision:** Don't talk about "React" or "Swift." Talk about "Cinematic experiences," "Investor-ready UI," and "User retention."
3.  **The Follow-up:** If they don't reply by Day 3 morning, send a polite "bump."
