These are your highest-probability leads. They are early enough to need you, but niche enough to value your "Cinematic" style.

## 1. VibeCheck (AI Mood Tracker)
*   **The Niche:** Mental Health / AI
*   **The Founder:** Search for "VibeCheck AI Founder" on Twitter/X.
*   **The Gap:** Currently uses a basic "Discord-style" UI. Needs a premium, glowing iOS app feel to stand out.
